Greetings in the name of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ!

It is with immense joy and gratitude that we welcome you to the digital home of our church. Whether you’re a long-standing member, a friend we’ve yet to meet in person, or a virtual visitor from across the globe, we are delighted to have you with us.

At TWM, our mission is rooted in love, guided by faith, and inspired by the teachings of Christ. This website serves as a gateway to the heart of our community—a space where we share the good news, stay connected, and extend the love of Christ beyond the walls of our physical sanctuary.

Thank you for being a part of our online community. We look forward to the day we can welcome you in person, but until then, know that you are in our prayers, and our digital doors are always open.

In Christ’s love
